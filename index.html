Your Story, Revised

Do you have a manuscript you’ve been longing to move from that secret drawer into the light of day?

Is there an Op-Ed you want to write related to a current event about which you are passionate, but are searching for just the right words?

Have you always wanted to write your life story, but don’t know where or how to begin? At Blueprint, offer a full-service manuscript consulting service for fiction and nonfiction of all stripes, and at most levels of completion and / or polish. Drop us a line at blueprintmanuscript@gmail.com.

Why Blueprint?

Our expert coaching and editorial services will help you move your manuscript from mundane to marvelous. We offer precise, practical notes; professional editorial letters that address central issues of craft (voice, structure, tone, plot, etc.) , and a quick turn-around time for these in-depth and comprehensive critiques.

Working with Blueprint
Before we take on new clients, we assess your needs, your skill level, and your hopes for your project. We also take good care to be sure that your project is a match for our unique skills and particular expertise, and we try to offer referrals to other services and private readers in the case that we are not a match. (Please note: we are not grammarians!) If we feel we can help you, we will put together a contract that is mutually agreed upon by all parties.
Our Promise, Our Contract
As working writers ourselves, we know how capricious this business can be, and we know that it’s not always a meritocracy, and that art is subjective. Writing is a vocation, an art form, a discipline and a practice. What we can promise is that we will do our very best to help you meet your goals, and to make your book or article or project as good as it can be. We are not literary agents, and we cannot guarantee that you will sell your book for a million dollars. Nobody can promise that, and if they do, they’re lying. We CANNOT promise to make you rich and famous, but we CAN promise to help you become the best writer you can be. Stories Are Important
We strongly believe that given the current political climate in the United States and around the world, stories are more important than ever before. Stories teach us empathy and compassion; they provide a space for levity and sadness, and they make room for grace. For that reason, we try to work with people, regardless of budget constraints. If you have a story you are passionate to tell, or a book you’ve been waiting your whole life to write, we will customize a plan of writerly action – a blueprint — that is uniquely suited to your unique story.

Meet Blueprint

Blueprint Manuscript Consulting is the love child of Emily Rapp Black and Kent Black. We're lucky we get to work with some other very talented writers.

Emily Rapp Black

Emily is the author of Poster Child (Bloomsbury USA) and The Still Point of the Turning World (Penguin Press). She is an Assistant Professor at the University of California-Riverside. Learn more at emilyrappblack.com.

Kent Black

Kent has been a working journalist for 35 years. A native Californian, educated at the University of California-Berkeley, Kent is the former Editor-in-Chief of Outside’s Go and Palm Springs Life. He also served as deputy editor for the New York Times Style section, and has been a contributing editor for Men’s Journal and Details.

Julia Goldberg

Julia is a writer and editor based in Santa Fe, NM, and author of the book Inside Story: Everyone’s Guide to Reporting and Writing Creative Nonfiction (Leaf Storm Press). She also serves as president of The New Mexico Fund for Public Interest Journalism. Julia can be found online at juliargolderg.com.

Bernadette Murphy

Bernadette has published four books of narrative nonfiction, most recently Harley and Me: Embracing Risk on the Road to a More Authentic Life (Counterpoint Press). Find Bernadette online at bernadette-murphy.com.
